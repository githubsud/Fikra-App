
How to Check the API Response
In your browser, with the app open, press F12 to open Developer Tools.
Go to the "Network" tab.
Submit an idea. You will see a new request appear in the list, likely named ideas. Click on it.
A new panel will open. Click on the "Response" or "Preview" tab. This shows you the exact JSON data that your frontend received from the backend for the list of ideas.
Look at the entry for your newest idea. Does it have an ai_enhanced_text field? Is the value an empty string (""), or is the field missing completely?
